Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes,cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as mushrooms,onions,olives, meat,etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Firm mozzarella cheese
  • Feta cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Italian pepperoncini
  • Italian sausage
  • Sliced black olives

How to Make Pizza

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree celsius for 10 minutes
