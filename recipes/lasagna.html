Lasagna



Description

Lasagna, a type of pasta , possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings such as vegetables, cheeses, and spices.


Layers

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat Sauce
  • Layer together

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
